Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common queries

How long does the adoption process take? +
Typically 3–7 days. We do a quick home check and match you with the right dog.
Can I volunteer on weekends only? +
Absolutely! We have weekend shifts. Sign up via our Volunteer page and pick slots that suit you.
Are donations tax-deductible? +
Yes. We are a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit. You'll get a receipt for every donation.
How can I report a stray dog? +
Use the Rescue & Medical page or call us directly. We respond to rescue requests within 2 hours.
