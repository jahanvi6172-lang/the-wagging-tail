Our Story

In 2018, three college friends noticed a big problem. Dozens of dog shelters across India were doing incredible work — but nobody knew about them. People who wanted to adopt had no idea where to go. Volunteers had no idea who needed help. Donors had no idea who to trust. The connection was missing.

So they built The Wagging Tail — one platform where every verified shelter, every available dog, and every person who wants to help can find each other instantly. We don't rescue dogs. The shelters do. We just make sure their work reaches the world.
