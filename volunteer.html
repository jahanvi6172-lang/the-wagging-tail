Emergency Rescue Volunteer

Time: On call basis

When an injured animal is reported, you get notified. You pick up the animal and transport it to the nearest verified vet.

Medical Support Volunteer

Time: Flexible

Assist verified vets during treatment of rescued animals. Help with post-treatment care and rehabilitation of rescued animals.

Animals recover faster with dedicated care and attention.
